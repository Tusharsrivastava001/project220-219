Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and plans

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For annual plans, we also accept checks and wire transfers.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When you upgrade, you'll only pay the prorated difference for the remaining billing period. Downgrades will take effect at your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all plans with no credit card required. You'll have full access to all features during the trial period.

What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

We'll notify you if you're approaching your plan limits. For product limits, you'll need to upgrade to continue adding more products. For other limits like bandwidth, we offer pay-as-you-go options or you can upgrade your plan.
